Chicken with Mustard Cream Sauce & Spinach Salad

Decription:

Tasty refreshing salad to get you going...

Ingredients:

  • 1 (5 oz) pkg baby spinach
  • ¾ lb chicken breasts, boneless skinless
  • 4 fl oz chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 shallot
  • black pepper
  • butter, unsalted
  • Dijon mustard
  • salt
  • white wine vinegar

Steps:

  1. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Slice chicken in half horizontally to form thin fillets. Season with salt and pepper on both sides. ¾ lb chicken breasts, boneless skinless ¼ tsp salt ⅛ tsp black pepper
  3. Add butter to skillet and swirl to coat bottom. Add chicken and cook until browned and cooked through, 2-3 minutes per side. Transfer to a plate. ½ tbsp butter, unsalted
  4. Peel and mince shallot. 1 shallot
  5. Once chicken has been transferred, reduce heat to medium. Add more butter and shallot; cook, stirring frequently, until softened, 2-3 minutes. ½ tbsp butter, unsalted
  6. Add broth and vinegar; scrape skillet to loosen any browned bits. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the liquid is reduced by about half, 4-5 minutes. 4 fl oz (½ cup) chicken or vegetable broth 2 tbsp white wine vinegar
  7. Add Dijon and butter; stir until well combined and continue cooking for 1-2 minutes. Remove from heat and season with salt and freshly ground pepper. 1 tbsp Dijon mustard 2 tbsp butter, unsalted ⅛ tsp salt ⅛ tsp black pepper
  8. Wash and dry spinach. 1 (5 oz) pkg baby spinach
  9. To serve, arrange spinach and chicken on a plate; drizzle with mustard sauce and enjoy!
